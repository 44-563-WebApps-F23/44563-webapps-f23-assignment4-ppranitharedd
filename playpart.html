HILDA.[Stares fixedly upwards and says, as if petrified.] My Master Builder. RAGNAR.[Supports himself, trembling, against the railing.] He must be dashed to pieces--killed on the spot. ONE OF THE LADIES.[Whilst MRS. SOLNESS is carried into the house.] Run down for the doctor-- RAGNAR.I can't stir a root-- ANOTHER LADY.Then call to some one! RAGNAR.[Tries to call out.] How is it? Is he alive? A VOICE.[Below, in the garden.] Mr. Solness is dead! OTHER VOICES.[Nearer.] The head is all crushed.--he fell right into the quarry. HILDA.[Turns to RAGNAR, and says quietly.] I can't see him up there now. RAGNAR. This is terrible. So, after all, he could not do it. HILDA.[As if in quiet spell-bound triumph.] But he mounted right to the top. And I heard harps in the air. [Waves her shawl in the air, and shrieks with wild intensity.] My--my Master Builder!
